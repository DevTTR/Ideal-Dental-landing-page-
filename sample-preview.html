FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

Do you accept my insurance? +
We work with many major PPO insurance plans and help maximize your benefits. Call us to verify your coverage and we'll handle the paperwork for you.
Do you offer financing? +
Yes, flexible payment options are available for qualified patients. We offer various financing plans to make dental care affordable. Ask us about current financing options.
Are same-day appointments available? +
We hold space for urgent needs and emergencies. When possible, we'll see you the same day. Call us as early as possible for best availability.
What should I bring to my first appointment? +
Please bring your insurance card, a valid ID, and a list of any medications you're currently taking. If you have previous dental X-rays, we can request those from your former dentist.
